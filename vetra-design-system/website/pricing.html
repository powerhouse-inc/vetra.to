Frequently asked questions

‣ Can I try Pro before committing?
Absolutely. Every new workspace starts with a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. When the trial ends, you can choose to subscribe or move to the free Starter plan with no data loss.
‣ How does per-member pricing work?
You are billed only for active members who use the workspace. Guest users, viewers, and deactivated accounts are never charged. If a member is removed mid-cycle, we prorate the remaining balance as a credit on your next invoice.
‣ Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?
Yes. You can switch to annual billing at any time from your workspace settings. When you switch, the remaining value of your current monthly period is applied as a prorated credit toward the annual plan.
‣ What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data is never deleted. If you downgrade to Starter, features like automations and custom fields become read-only. You can still view everything, but creating new automations or fields requires upgrading back to Pro.
‣ Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes. We offer 50% off Pro pricing for registered nonprofits and accredited educational institutions. Reach out to our sales team with documentation and we will apply the discount within one business day.
‣ Is my data secure?
Security is foundational to Vetra. We are SOC 2 Type II certified, encrypt all data at rest and in transit, and undergo regular third-party penetration tests. Enterprise customers can also choose data residency in the US, EU, or APAC regions.
