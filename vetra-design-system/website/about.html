About Vetra

We believe every team deserves tools that are as thoughtful as the products they build. Vetra was founded to eliminate the friction between great ideas and shipped software, giving product teams a single, elegant workspace to plan, collaborate, and deliver with confidence.

Our values

The principles that guide everything we build and every decision we make.

⚖

Clarity over complexity

We strip away noise so teams can focus on what matters. Every feature we ship must make workflows simpler, not more complicated. If it adds cognitive load, it does not ship.

⚡

Speed as a feature

Performance is not an afterthought. From sub-100ms interactions to instant search, we obsess over speed because your team's momentum depends on it.

❤

Craft in every detail

Great software is built by people who care about the details. We sweat the micro-interactions, the edge cases, and the quiet moments that make a tool feel truly right.

Our journey

Key milestones on the path to building the future of product management.

2020

The idea takes shape

Co-founders Elena Vasquez and James Park, frustrated by juggling five different tools at their previous startup, sketch out the first wireframes for a unified product workspace over a weekend hackathon.

2021

Seed round & first users

Vetra raises a $4M seed round led by Horizon Ventures. The private beta launches with 30 design-partner teams, and early feedback shapes the core planning and collaboration features.

2022

Public launch

Vetra goes live on Product Hunt, reaching #1 Product of the Day. Over 2,000 teams sign up in the first month. The platform adds integrations with GitHub, Slack, and Figma.

2024

Series A & enterprise expansion

With 12,000+ teams on the platform, Vetra raises a $28M Series A led by Meridian Capital. The enterprise tier launches with SSO, SCIM, and advanced audit logging.

2026

AI-powered workflows

Vetra introduces intelligent automation features powered by proprietary AI models, enabling teams to auto-generate specs, triage bugs, and predict delivery timelines with remarkable accuracy.
